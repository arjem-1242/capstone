Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - PESO Sync
1. What is PESO Sync?

PESO Sync is an online platform designed to streamline job matching and employment services through the Public Employment Service Office (PESO). It connects job seekers, employers, and PESO staff to facilitate job postings, job profiling, and accreditation processes.

2. Who can use PESO Sync?

PESOSync is available for:

• Job Seekers – Individuals looking for job opportunities.

• Employers – Companies or businesses looking to hire employees.

• PESO Staff – Government personnel managing job fairs, job postings, and employer accreditation.
3. How do I create an account on PESOSync?

You can register based on your role:

• Job Seekers: Fill out the registration form with your details and upload your resume.

• Employers: Submit company details and wait for PESO accreditation.

• PESO Staff: Accounts are created by the system administrator.
4. What is the role of PESO staff in PESO Sync?

PESO staff manage employer accreditation, oversee job postings, and ensure job seekers have access to relevant opportunities. They also handle job fair events and analyze employment data.

5. How do I approve an employer's accreditation?

Log in to your PESO staff account, go to Employer Requests, review the submitted details, and approve or reject the accreditation request.

6. Can PESO staff generate reports from the system?

Yes, PESOSync provides analytics and reports on job applications, employer activity, and labor market trends.

Technical & Support Questions
1. I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

Click on Forgot Password? on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

2. Can I use PESOSync on my mobile phone?

Yes, PESOSync is mobile-friendly and can be accessed through any web browser on your smartphone.
