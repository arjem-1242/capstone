About Us

Empowering Job Seekers, Connecting Employers, and Building Stronger Communities

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) San Pedro

is a government initiative dedicated to connecting job seekers with employers, providing employment facilitation, career guidance, and job-matching services. As a key player in workforce development, PESO San Pedro organizes job fairs, skills training, and livelihood programs to enhance employability and support local businesses.

  • Helps individuals access job opportunities through employer connections, career guidance, and skills development programs.
  • Ensures companies find qualified talent through job matching and recruitment programs.
  • Committed to economic growth through workforce development and job creation.

PESO San Pedro serves as a vital resource for both job seekers and employers in the city by providing employment opportunities, career guidance, and skills development programs.

01

Job Placement Services

Helping job seekers connect with potential employers through job matching and referrals.

02

Career Guidance & Counseling

Providing career advice, resume-building support, and interview preparation to enhance employability.

03

Job Fairs

Organizing job fairs and recruitment events to facilitate direct hiring opportunities.

04

Labor Market Information

Offering up-to-date insights on employment trends, in-demand jobs, and salary expectations.

05

Company Accreditation

Ensuring that employers comply with PESO regulations for legitimate and fair hiring practices.

06

Special Program for Employment of Students (SPES)

Providing work opportunities for students to help them gain experience and financial assistance for their education.
